# Sales Invoice

The Sales Invoice module enables the generation of invoices for goods or services delivered to customers. It integrates seamlessly with delivery notes and other sales processes for accurate billing and record-keeping.

## Accessing Sales Invoice Dashboard

>     Navigate to Sale > Sales Invoice.

<img src="../images/sales invoice dashboard.png" alt="sales invoice dashboard" style="border-radius: 10px; width: 70%; height: 70%; border: 0.5px solid #333;">

The Sales Invoice Dashboard will appear, displaying: All sales invoices categorized as Draft or Approved. There will be options to Search or use Advanced Filters for refined results.

## Export to Excel

Export all sales invoices by clicking the Export to Excel button.

## Saving and Approving Sales Invoice

Save: Once all details are entered, click Save to create the invoice.
Approve: After saving, click Approve to finalize the invoice.
