# Bank Account Management

Easily manage company and personal bank accounts in the Trading ERP. This guide explains how to view existing bank accounts and create a new bank account for transactional and accounting purposes.

---

## Accessing Bank Account Management

### Step 1: Navigate to **Accounting > Bank Accounts**

This page opens the **Bank Account Dashboard**, where you can:
- View all existing bank accounts
- Identify company and personal accounts
- Use bank accounts for payments, receipts, and accounting transactions


<img src="../images/Bank Accounts Dashboard.PNG" alt="Bank Account Dashboard" style="border-radius: 10px; width: 70%; height: auto; border: 1px solid #333;">

---

## Viewing Existing Bank Accounts

Once you enter the **Bank Accounts** menu:

- All previously created bank accounts will be listed
- Each bank account is linked with a **Chart of Account (COA)**
- These accounts can be used for:
  - Payments
  - Receipts
  - Journal entries
  - Bank-related transactions

---

## Adding a New Bank Account

To add a new bank account, follow the steps below.

### Step 1: Click **Create New**

This opens the **Create New Bank Account** form where you can enter banking and accounting details.


<img src="../images/Create Bank Accounts.PNG" alt="Create New Bank Account" style="border-radius: 10px; width: 70%; height: auto; border: 1px solid #333;">

---

## Bank Account Creation Form

### 1. Basic Bank Details

| Field | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Bank Type** | Select one of the following: Current, Savings, Local, International |
| **Currency** | Select the currency for the bank account |
| **Bank Account Name** | Enter a recognizable account name |
| **Bank Account Number** | Enter the bank account number |
| **Bank Name** | Enter the bank name |
| **Bank Branch** | Enter the branch name |
| **Bank Contact Number** | Optional contact number |

---

### 2. Account & Identification Details

| Field | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Bank Account Type** | Specify the internal account classification |
| **IBAN** | Enter the IBAN number (if applicable) |
| **Bank Code** | Enter bank routing or identification code |
| **Swift Number** | Required for international bank accounts |

---

### 3. Relationship Officer Details (Optional)

| Field | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Relationship Officer Name** | Name of the bank relationship officer |
| **Relationship Officer Contact Number** | Contact number of the officer |

---

### 4. Maintenance & Ownership Details

| Field | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Maintained By** | Select the responsible user from the dropdown |
| **Is Company Account** | Enable if the bank account belongs to the company |
| **Is Default Account** | Set this account as the default bank account (optional) |

---

### 5. Chart of Account (COA) Configuration

| Option | Description |
|------|------------|
| **Auto Generate COA** | Automatically creates and maps a Chart of Account for this bank account |
| **Manual COA Mapping** | Disable auto generation to manually select a COA from the dropdown |

> **Note:**  
> - When **Is Company Account** and **Auto Generate COA** are enabled, the system automatically creates and maps the Chart of Account.
> - If auto generation is disabled, manual COA selection is mandatory.

---

## Saving the Bank Account

### Step 2: Click **Save**

- The bank account will be created successfully
- A Chart of Account will be created or mapped based on your configuration
- The bank account will be available for:
  - Payments
  - Receipts
  - Transfers
  - Accounting and financial transactions

---

**Congratulations!**  
You have successfully created a bank account in the Trading ERP and it is now ready for transactional use.
